No Bake Cheesecake

Description

This is an amazing recipe for cheesecake with whipping cream and cream cheese that I just threw together one day — no oven, no eggs. Everyone loves it so much, that it doesn't last long. This is good with any kind of canned or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ⅓ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Directions

  1. Step 1: Stir together graham cracker crumbs, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Add melted butter and mix well. Press into the bottom of an 8- or 10-inch springform pan. Chill until firm.

  2. Step 2: Beat together cream cheese and lemon juice in a medium bowl until soft. Add whipping cream and beat with an electric mixer until the batter becomes thick. Add sugar and continue to beat until stiff.

  3. Step 3: Pour into chilled crust, and top with pie filling. Chill for several hours or overnight.

  4. Just before serving, remove the sides of the springform pan.
